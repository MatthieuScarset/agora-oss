# Mission

Build an agnostic, agent-enabled platform that turns FLOSS community data into
clear insights, useful data products, and actionable recommendations.

## Problem to solve

Community data is spread across multiple sources, hard to normalize, and costly
to analyze manually. Maintainers need a way to quickly understand activity,
identify trends, and act on signals without stitching together custom scripts.

## Audience

- Maintainers who need practical insight into project health
- Community members and contributors who want visibility into participation
- Researchers and analytics consumers who need reusable, normalized data

## Success criteria

The mission is successful when Agora can:

- ingest data from multiple FLOSS sources through a common pipeline
- normalize raw records into stable Agora models
- build data products that support dashboard views and semantic search
- surface insights that are understandable and actionable for users
- remain extensible for new providers, entities, and agent workflows

## Methodology

We use agentic workflows, data-driven DDD, spec-driven development, and BDD to
keep the system grounded in concrete user outcomes and testable behavior.
